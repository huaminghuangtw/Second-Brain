* 3Q Filters Test:
If you want to curb stress, you need to curate your thoughts.
Run them through Jon Acuff's 3 filters:
• Is it true?
• Is it helpful?
• Is it kind?
If they're not accurate, not serving you and making you feel worse...
Discard them immediately.
