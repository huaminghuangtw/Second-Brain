"真正的窮，不是沒有錢，而是沒有能力去付出。相對的，富裕是什麼?不是誰擁有得多，而是誰需要的少。" — 沈芯菱

Your true wealth is the quality of your attention. What you pay attention to—expands.

You should only have to get rich once, and if you are going to swing for the fences, you still have to be sure that once you hit it, you will never bet it black again.

Wealth is created not by spending your time making money but rather by saving your time to make money.
