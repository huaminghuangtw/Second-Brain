When you say 'yes' to others, make sure you are not saying 'no' to yourself.

[The "Yes-Damn" Effect](https://www.instagram.com/reel/CxDR2m2gFAt/)
- We often say yes to things assuming that we will have more time or energy for them in the future.
- As a rule of thumb, if you're going to say yes to anything because you think you'll have more time or energy for it in the future, say no instead!

"Say Yes, Then No"
> You should say yes frequently as you explore the possibilities, and then say no frequently as you double down on what works.
