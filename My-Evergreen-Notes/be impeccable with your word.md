Always be aware of how you are using the word, and be *impeccable* with your word. Avoid using the word to [[self-talk|speak against yourself]] or to gossip about others.

Speak with integrity. Say only what you mean.
- People can't read your thoughts. If you think something, say something. Don't expect people to guess your thoughts and feelings. Don't hold a grudge when people didn't predict what you wanted them to do. Just tell them.

多聽少說: Make "[[Active Listening]] Mode" your default.

THINK before you SPEAK(, and READ before you THINK.)

"Raise your words, not voice. It is rain that grows flowers, not thunder."

Some questions to consider before you speak:
 - "*Does this need to be said?*"
 - "*Does this need to be said by me?*"
 - "*Does this need to be said by me right now?*"
