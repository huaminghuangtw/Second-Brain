The phenomenon of "'empathic stress" causes individuals who are exposed to people who are stressed to experience stress themselves.

If someone feels **compassion ("I want to relieve your suffering.")** for a stressed person instead of **empathy ("I feel your suffering.")**, they may be less likely to take on that person's stress.

TSST = Trier Social Stress Test

MBSR = Mindfulness-Based [[rest, relax, recharge|Stress Reduction]]
