- The Psychology of Future Self
- "Human beings are works in progress that mistakenly think they're finished. *We somehow imagine that the person we are right now is the person we'll be for the rest of time.* Hint: that's not the case. The person you are right now is as transient, as fleeting, and as temporary as all the people you've ever been. One constant in our life is change." — Dan Gilbert
- People tend to underestimate how much they will change in the future, despite knowing how much they have changed over time.
