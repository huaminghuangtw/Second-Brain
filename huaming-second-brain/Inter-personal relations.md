- The most important conversations you have in life are the ones you have with yourself. Make them constructive. Treat yourself with respect.
- The ability to love yourself improves your ability to be loved.
	- We are unlikely to accept a relationship that is worse than the one we have with ourselves, and thus the person who is [[Happiness|happy]] and comfortable with themselves is in a great position. The person with healthy self-esteem doesn't have to jump into any relationship because they already have a great one wherever they go.
